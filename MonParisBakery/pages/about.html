About


Established in 2015. Andre' Gratesol is a famous French baker who came back to Reflections Lakes Plaza with a great variety of baked goods, an excellent coffee and tea selection, delicious sandwiches, and other products. The new ownership is trying to bring back the French Bakery in its best to this upscale S.Fort Myers community.



Located conveniently at the SW intersection of Cypress Lake Drive and Reflections Pkwy behind Jasons Deli in the Arcade (plenty of parking out in front of the shop).
